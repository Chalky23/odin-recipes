Low FODMAP Poke Bowl

Description

This alternative to an Asian inspired poke bowl will suit all those who need to follow the Low FODMAP diet.

All ingredients are available to be switched out to suit your own diet and tastes.

Ingredients

  • Rice (cooked or packet rice)
  • Prawns (or alternative protein)
  • Sweetcorn
  • Spring onion
  • Bok choi
  • Oil (try garlic or sesame for flavour)
  • Lime
  • Ginger paste
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt, pepper, chinese five spice.

Steps

  1. In a wok, heat your oil.
  2. Fry off your protein with chinese five spice.
  3. Add your vegetables and cook for a further two minutes.
  4. Add the desired amount of ginger and lime.
  5. Mix through your rice and add the soy sauce.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste.
